Building Specification - Block A
Systems:
- HVAC: 2 chillers, 6 AHUs, 12 VAV zones
- Pumps: CP-1, CP-2, CP-3 (primary loop), DP-1 (domestic)
- Energy Metering: EM-1 per floor, EM-2 per chiller
Sensor Naming:
- temp.Z{zone}.C
- vib.PUMP{n}.mm_s
- power.AHU{k}.kW
- flow.CP{n}.m3h
Setpoints:
- Zone Temperature: 23.5 ± 1.0 C
- Pump Vibration Alarm: > 7.1 mm/s RMS (ISO 10816)
- AHU Power Baseline: 3.5–5.0 kW at 60% speed
